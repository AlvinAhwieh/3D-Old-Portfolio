In To Space

In To Space is a 2D endless runner game where the objective is to maneuver your spaceship through space while dodging asteroids, missiles, and other spaceships.

The game draws inspiration from the 8-bit era, both in terms of sound design and graphics. In To Space's general gameplay loop utilizes a pool of strings, with each letter representing an obstacle. These strings are randomly called once the previous one ends, creating interesting and unique playthroughs each time.
