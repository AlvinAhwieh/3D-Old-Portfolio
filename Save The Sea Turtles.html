Save The Sea Turtles

Save The Sea Turtles is an endless survival first-person shooter game. In the game, you are tasked with defending sea turtles and their eggs. The player is equipped with a bubble gun. Enemies emerge from the fog with the mission of reaching the turtles and their eggs. The game features a power-up system to aid the player in surviving as long as possible.

The game was created as part of my final year project for university. The project aimed to explore the effectiveness of using a species endangerment game to raise awareness.
