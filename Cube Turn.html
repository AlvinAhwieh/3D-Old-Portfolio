Cube Turn

Cube Turn invites players to excersize their reflexes and cognitives skills. A brand new refreshing take on the puzzle game genre. One that will have you coming back again and again trying to beat your highscore.

Cube Turn is a puzzle game involving a cube with 6 sides as well as some tiles on the floor. The tiles on the floor change colour depending on the colours on the side of the cube. The Player has to match the colours on the side of the cube to the ones on the floor, with a new colour appearing after some time. If the player fails to keep up or falls off the side then the player dies and they are given a score.
